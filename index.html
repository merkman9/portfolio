About

I'm a photographer, multimedia artist, musician, and event curator driven by curiosity and a passion for pushing creative boundaries. Since childhood, I’ve explored digital and physical art forms such as computer graphics and immersive installations that blend technology, sound, and light. My work often reimagines scale and perception, inviting audiences into surreal, sensory-rich environments. My recent projects, like Music for Ants, explore themes of cosmic insignificance through interactive projections and spatial audio. Whether building visuals from scratch or collaborating across disciplines, I’m interested in art that feels both otherworldly and deeply human, and creating experiences that alter perspective and transmit emotion without explanation.
